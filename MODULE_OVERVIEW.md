# Schrö — Module Overview

A full breakdown of every module in the Schrö system: its purpose, core mechanics, and architecture.

---

## 🏎️ Control Centre
**Purpose:** Daily operations and system health monitor.
**Key Features:**
- **Engine Velocity**: Dynamic efficiency gauge (productivity score).
- **Daily Brief**: AI-generated summary of upcoming tasks and priorities.
- **Performance**: High-level visualisation of health, finance, and task metrics.
**Status:** Active.

---

## 🛠️ Operations
**Purpose:** Centralised task and event management.
**Sub-modules:**
- **Planner**: Algorithmic timeline for daily scheduling.
- **Deployment**: Core todo list for Personal and Business profiles.
- **Reminders**: Time-sensitive notification triggers.
- **Groceries**: Smart shopping lists.
- **Focus Map**: Spatial X/Y grid for strategic task placement.
**Mechanics:** Uses Google Maps API for travel times and Gemini AI for priority scoring.
**Status:** Active.

---

## 🧠 Intelligence
**Purpose:** AI-driven strategy and assistance.
**Key Features:**
- **Personas**: Targeted identities (Ruby, Vance, Kael) for specialized advice.
- **Neural Voice**: Multimodal interaction with HD low-latency voice.
- **Data Querying**: AI has secure access to system-wide data modules.
**Status:** Active (Advanced).

---

## 💰 Finances
**Purpose:** Financial operating system for Personal and Business entities.
**Mechanics:**
- **Projections**: Real-time forecasting based on bills and income.
- **Transactions**: Secure ledger with automated categorization.
- **Assets & Liabilities**: Tracking for savings pots and debts.
**Status:** Active.

---

## 🛡️ Vault
**Purpose:** Secure storage for sensitive digital assets.
**Key Features:**
- **Clipboard**: Rich-text manager with PIN-lock protection.
- **Secrets**: Encrypted credential and API key storage.
**Status:** Active.

---

## ✨ Studio
**Purpose:** Research, development, and portfolio construction.
**Key Features:**
- **Canvas**: Visual thought map and node connection system.
- **Projects**: Milestone-based tracking for long-term initiatives.
- **Content**: Strategy and scheduling for media production.
**Status:** Active.

---

## 🎯 Manifest
**Purpose:** Long-term intention and dream tracking.
**Key Features:**
- **Targets**: Hard strategic goals linked to daily operations.
- **Dreams**: High-level aspirations for future planning.
**Status:** Active.

---

## 💚 Wellbeing
**Purpose:** Holistic physical and mental health optimization.
**Key Features:**
- **Fitness**: Workout logging integrated with routine libraries.
- **Nutrition**: Meal tracking and inventory management.
- **Mind**: Mood logging and emotional health analytics.
**Status:** Active.

---

## ⚙️ System Control
**Purpose:** OS-level configuration and maintenance.
**Features:** Profile management, theme engines, demo mode, and deployment roadmaps.
**Status:** Active.
